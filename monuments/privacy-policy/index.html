Monuments Tour Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Monuments Tour is developed by TravelPal LLC. This policy describes how Monuments Tour handles your data.

Data Collection

Monuments Tour does not collect, store, or sell any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

Internet Connection

Monuments Tour connects to the internet solely to deliver travel guides, itineraries, and curated content. This connection is used only to fetch app content — no personal data is transmitted.

No Tracking

Monuments Tour does not use advertising identifiers, third-party analytics, or any form of user tracking.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, it is used only on-device to show nearby points of interest. Location data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling Monuments Tour removes all locally stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Monuments Tour does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
