Frequently Asked Questions

What is Monuments Tour?

Monuments Tour is a travel guide app that provides personalized itineraries, expert city guides, and a 24/7 AI-powered tour guide. It combines human-curated travel expertise with responsible AI to make art and history accessible to every traveler.

How do I get a personalized itinerary?

Open the app, choose your destination or a nearby city, and the app will generate a personalized itinerary based on your interests. You can customize it further by selecting specific monuments, museums, or points of interest.

How do I use the city guides?

Tap the Guide tab to browse city guides. Each guide is curated by travel specialists and sourced from verified official channels. Tap any monument or landmark to get detailed information, history, and tips.

Does the app work offline?

Monuments Tour requires an internet connection to load guides and itineraries. We recommend downloading content for your destination before traveling to areas with limited connectivity.

Is my data private?

Yes. Monuments Tour does not collect or store any personal information. No account is required. The app connects to the internet only to deliver travel content. See our full Privacy Policy.

How is AI used in the app?

We use responsible AI technology to process and personalize travel information at scale. Every guide is carefully reviewed by real travel specialists and art historians. All AI usage is transparently labeled within the app.
