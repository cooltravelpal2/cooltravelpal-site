Discover places through stories and quests.

Museum Knight is an interactive guide that turns museums, landmarks, parks, and city walks into story quests — follow the clues, unlock each stop, and hear the tale behind what you're looking at.

Download on the App Store Read our Experiences guides

iPhone · Indoor museum quests and outdoor city, park & trail quests

How a quest works

1 · Choose a quest

Pick a guided experience at a museum, landmark, park, or neighborhood — each one is a story told through a sequence of real stops.

2 · Follow the clues

Each stop is revealed through hints and clues that guide you to the next work, monument, or viewpoint — discovery, not a checklist.

3 · Unlock the story

Arrive at a stop and its tale unlocks — narrated audio stories about the art, history, and hidden details in front of you.

An interactive travel guide, not just a game

✦

Quests

Single guided experiences — a museum wing, a landmark cluster, a park trail, or a city walk — with stops, clues, and rewards.

✦

Sagas

Larger story arcs that link quests across a destination, so a whole city unfolds as one progression.

✦

Indoor experiences

Museum quests built for halls and collections — find the work, hear its story, move to the next room with purpose.

✦

Outdoor experiences

City, park, and trail quests that use the map to guide you between landmarks and reveal each stop on arrival.

✦

Audio narration

Character-voiced tales — researched, story-driven, and about three minutes each — so you can look while you listen.

✦

Maps & progression

Quest maps track where you've been and what's still locked, turning a visit into an unfolding story.
