About Cool TravelPal

Cool TravelPal is a travel publication and toolkit from TravelPal LLC — practical guides for travelers, plus privacy-first iOS apps that put the advice to work.

What we do

We write practical, evergreen guides across three connected pillars — travel planning, credit-card rewards, and destination experiences — and we build the apps we wished existed for each one:

  • TravelOrbit keeps a trip organized and guides the travel day itself.
  • CardPecker answers "which card should I use?" and tracks benefits before they expire.
  • Museum Knight — a new way to experience museums, landmarks, and cities. Coming soon.

How we think about privacy

Travel data is personal — itineraries, documents, spending patterns, location. Our apps are local-first: your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud, there are no ad trackers, and none of our apps ask for bank credentials. The same restraint applies to this website, which uses no invasive tracking.

Editorial independence

Our guides exist to be useful, not to sell placements. We don't run sponsored card recommendations, and our Cards & Points writing stays brand-neutral education. Where our own apps are relevant, we say so openly — they're clearly ours.

The company

Cool TravelPal is published by TravelPal LLC. You can reach us at admin@cooltravelpal.com.
