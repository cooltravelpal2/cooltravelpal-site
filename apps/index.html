Three apps for three parts of the journey

Cool TravelPal's apps are built on one belief: travel tools should be useful, calm, and private. Each app supports one stage of the journey — planning and travel day, paying for it well, and experiencing the destination.

TravelOrbit

Stay organized throughout your journey.

A real-time travel-day co-pilot. Every booking in one calm timeline, the next move surfaced when it matters — leave-by times, check-in, gates — with live flight updates on your Lock Screen, companion sharing, and a private on-device AI assistant.

iPhone · iPad · Apple Watch

CardPecker

Always know which card to use.

Compare rewards across your cards, track statement credits, card-linked offers, receipts, and points balances — and get more value from the wallet you already have. Private by design: no account, no bank logins, no tracking.
