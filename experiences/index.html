Quests, Sagas, and how they fit

✦

Quest

A single guided experience at one place — a museum wing, a landmark, a park trail, or a city walk — told through stops, clues, and audio narration.

✦

Saga

A larger story arc that links several quests across a destination, so a whole city or region unfolds as one progression.

✦

Indoor experiences

Museum and gallery quests designed for halls and collections — find the work, hear its story, unlock the next stop.

✦

Outdoor experiences

City-walk, park, and trail quests that use the map to guide you between landmarks and reveal each one's story on arrival.
