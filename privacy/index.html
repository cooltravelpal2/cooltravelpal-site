Website Privacy Policy

This policy covers cooltravelpal.com and the Cool TravelPal newsletter. The separate privacy policies linked from each app page cover our mobile applications.

Last updated July 15, 2026

Information you choose to provide

If you subscribe to the newsletter, EmailOctopus processes your email address so that TravelPal LLC can send the updates you requested, manage subscriptions, and honor unsubscribe requests. The signup form uses Google reCAPTCHA to reduce automated spam; Google may process technical information under its own privacy policy and terms. If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

Website analytics

Cool TravelPal may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, such as pages viewed, general traffic sources, device category, and interactions with important links. We configure analytics without advertising personalization or Google Signals and do not send newsletter form contents or email addresses to analytics. Google may process technical information such as IP address, browser information, and identifiers under its own privacy terms.

Hosting and technical logs

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages. Like most hosting providers, GitHub may process technical request information needed to operate, secure, and diagnose the service.

External links

Links to app stores, booking services, retailers, social networks, or other websites take you to services governed by their own privacy policies. Future affiliate or referral links will be clearly disclosed where they appear.

Retention and choices

Newsletter subscribers can unsubscribe using the link in any newsletter. You may also email us to ask about, correct, or delete information you provided directly to us, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to admin@cooltravelpal.com.
