Introducing CardPecker

A simple, private app that tells you which credit card to use for every purchase — no account required, and no data ever leaves your device.

By the Cool TravelPal Team Published April 4, 2026 Updated April 4, 2026 3 min read

We're excited to announce CardPecker — a simple, private app that tells you which credit card to use for every purchase.

If you carry more than one rewards card, you know the checkout-line math: is this "dining" or "entertainment"? Which card has the grocery multiplier this quarter? CardPecker replaces that guesswork. Add your cards, tap a spending category, and instantly see which card earns the most.

Built for real multi-card wallets

Subcategories let you distinguish the airlines and hotel brands that matter to your wallet — Delta from United, Hyatt from Marriott — so the answer is right even when the category is specific. And the Coupon Book tracks your statement credits and recurring benefits, refreshing them each billing period so nothing expires unnoticed.

Private by design

CardPecker stores everything on your device. There is no account, no bank login, no internet connection, and no tracking of any kind. Your wallet is your business.

CardPecker is available now on the App Store — read more on the CardPecker page, or dive into our Beginner's Guide to Credit-Card Rewards to put it to work.

Track it in CardPecker

CardPecker keeps your cards, reward categories, and credits in one private, offline app — so the best card for a purchase, and every expiring benefit, is always one tap away.
