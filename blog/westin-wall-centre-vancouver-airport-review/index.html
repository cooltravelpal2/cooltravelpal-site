Richmond · Airport Hotel Review

Westin Wall Centre Vancouver Airport Review

This is a Richmond airport hotel, not a downtown Vancouver base. Choose it for YVR, Chinese dining, a reliable Westin room, and a complimentary shuttle—not for city sightseeing or guaranteed suite upgrades.

By the Cool TravelPal TeamPublished July 16, 20265 min read
Best forEarly YVR departures, late arrivals, Richmond dining
Strong pointsComplimentary reserved shuttle, comfortable rooms, service
Weak pointsNo lounge, paid parking, limited upgrade expectations

Location and shuttle

The hotel is at 3099 Corvette Way, about four kilometers from YVR. Marriott currently lists a complimentary airport shuttle and says reservations are required. Confirm the current pickup point and schedule directly—do not rely on an old PDF when a missed airport transfer matters.

Rooms and elite treatment

The rooms meet the North American Westin standard: comfortable bed, functional work area, mini-fridge, and restrained décor. Our elite stay received a higher floor rather than a suite. There is no executive lounge; the front desk provided complimentary nonalcoholic drinks during our stay, but that is not a published permanent substitute.

Breakfast and Richmond food

Our Platinum breakfast was à la carte at the apron with familiar omelet, pancake, and French-toast choices. Current Bonvoy breakfast benefits depend on brand, region, status, chosen welcome gift, and terms. Richmond’s Chinese restaurant concentration is a stronger reason to stay here than hotel dining alone.

Parking and value

Self-parking is currently listed at C$30 daily and valet at C$40. Compare the neighboring Vancouver Airport Marriott, cash rates, and award prices separately: the hotel with the lower cash price can still demand more points. For long-term airport parking, compare YVR and off-airport packages rather than assuming the hotel lot is cheapest.

This is independent and not sponsored. Shuttle schedules, reservations, parking, breakfast, elite benefits, and award prices change. Hotel facts were checked against Marriott on July 16, 2026.
