Planners vs. co-pilots

A planner organizes your trip.
TravelOrbit runs it.

Like a navigator for your whole journey — calm before, certain during.

01 — The moment engine

The next step, before you ask

One screen, one focus. TravelOrbit reads your itinerary and surfaces the single thing that matters right now — confirm bookings, pack, check in, leave home — then quietly advances as your day unfolds.

  • Leave-by timing
  • Auto-advancing timeline
  • Gentle countdowns
02 — Ask your trip

A travel assistant that runs on your phone

Ask in plain words — "When do I board?", "How long is my layover?", "Where am I staying Tuesday?" — and get an answer pulled straight from your itinerary. It runs entirely on-device with Apple Intelligence, so nothing about your trip ever leaves your phone.

  • Answers from your itinerary
  • On-device & private
  • No account, no cloud
03 — Travel day

Your travel day, minute by minute

On the day itself, TravelOrbit follows the route — flight number, gate, terminal, boarding estimate, and an offline map of the airport. Live flight updates and a Live Activity keep the countdown and any gate change right on your Lock Screen, so you don't have to open the app.

  • Live flight updates
  • Gate change on the Lock Screen
  • Offline route map
04 — One trip, everything

Every booking in one timeline

Flights, hotels, rental cars, ground transit and activities live together in a single, scrollable trip — with seats, room numbers, confirmation codes and times exactly where you expect them.

  • Flights & hotels
  • Cars & transit
  • Seats & confirmations
05 — Prepared

Packing, with context

A packing list that knows where you're going. Pair it with destination weather cues so the trip feels handled before you ever zip the bag.

  • Smart packing list
  • Destination weather
  • Pre-trip checklist
06 — Everyone aboard

Every traveler, every document

Keep the whole party in one place — names, seats, room assignments and travel documents — with passport and ID scans encrypted on device and never sent to a server.

  • Multi-traveler
  • Passport & ID
  • Encrypted on device
07 — Yours to keep

Share, import & sync

Add any moment to your calendar, share a trip as a file, import plans from a companion, and back everything up to iCloud — your travel data stays yours, on your devices.

  • Add to Calendar
  • Share & import trips
  • iCloud backup
🔒

Private by design

TravelOrbit is local-first. Your itinerary and documents live on your device and in your own iCloud — passport and ID scans are encrypted and never handed to us. Even the AI assistant runs on-device, so your questions and trip details never leave your phone. No account required to start.
