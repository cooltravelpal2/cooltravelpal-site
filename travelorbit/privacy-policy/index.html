TravelOrbit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

TravelOrbit is developed by TravelPal LLC. This policy describes how TravelOrbit handles your data.

Trip and Traveler Data

TravelOrbit stores the trips, flights, hotels, rental cars, travelers, documents, packing notes, and related details you enter locally on your device.

iCloud Drive Backup

If you choose to use backup and restore, TravelOrbit may write backup files to your iCloud Drive. You control those files through your Apple account and iCloud Drive settings.

Sharing Trips with Companions

TravelOrbit lets you invite other people to a trip so you can follow it together. Shared trips sync through Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) using your own iCloud account — TravelOrbit operates no servers and cannot access your trips or any shared content. When you invite someone, the email address or phone number you enter is passed to Apple to deliver the invitation; it is not stored or collected by TravelOrbit. Your passport and travel-document numbers are never shared with companions, even when you choose to share seats, confirmations, or passes. A companion can leave a shared trip at any time, and the trip's owner can stop sharing to remove everyone's access.

Calendar Access

TravelOrbit asks for Calendar access only when you choose to add trip events to Calendar. Calendar access is used to create the selected events.

Location Access

TravelOrbit may ask for location permission to help advance travel moments when you arrive at places such as an airport or hotel. You can change location access in iOS Settings.

Live Activities

TravelOrbit may use Live Activities to display travel-day information on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island when enabled.

TravelOrbit Plus and Live Flight Refresh

TravelOrbit Plus is planned for live flight refresh and airport detail updates. When live refresh is used, flight identifiers and trip timing needed to request flight status may be sent to flight data providers.

No Advertising or Tracking

TravelOrbit does not use your trip data for advertising. TravelOrbit does not sell your personal information.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes data stored locally on your device. If you created iCloud Drive backup files, you can delete those separately from iCloud Drive.

Children's Privacy

TravelOrbit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
