Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Open TravelPaw, add a trip, then enter the flights, hotels, rental cars, travelers, and details you want to track. TravelPaw builds a trip timeline from those items.

What are Home moment cards?

Moment cards show the next useful travel action, such as confirming plans, packing, checking in, going through security, boarding, arriving, or getting to your hotel.

How do I review the full itinerary?

Use Trip Preview from Home to review flights, hotels, rental cars, and other trip items in time order.

How does packing work?

TravelPaw includes packing notes and destination weather cues so you can prepare before the trip starts.

Can I store traveler documents?

Yes. You can keep traveler details and documents organized locally in the app. Use fake or masked values when sharing screenshots or exported plans.

Can I add my trip to Calendar?

Yes. TravelPaw can add selected flights, hotels, and trip events to Calendar after you grant Calendar access.

How do share and import work?

You can export a TravelPaw trip file and share it by AirDrop, Files, or other share options. Imported trips show a preview before you add them.

Does TravelPaw support backup and restore?

TravelPaw can back up and restore travel data using iCloud Drive when you choose to use that feature.

What is TravelPaw Plus?

TravelPaw Plus is planned for live flight refresh, airport detail updates, Live Activities, and future shared trip sync. Core trip planning features work locally.
