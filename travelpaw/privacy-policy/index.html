TravelPaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

TravelPaw is developed by TravelPal LLC. This policy describes how TravelPaw handles your data.

Trip and Traveler Data

TravelPaw stores the trips, flights, hotels, rental cars, travelers, documents, packing notes, and related details you enter locally on your device.

iCloud Drive Backup

If you choose to use backup and restore, TravelPaw may write backup files to your iCloud Drive. You control those files through your Apple account and iCloud Drive settings.

Calendar Access

TravelPaw asks for Calendar access only when you choose to add trip events to Calendar. Calendar access is used to create the selected events.

Location Access

TravelPaw may ask for location permission to help advance travel moments when you arrive at places such as an airport or hotel. You can change location access in iOS Settings.

Live Activities

TravelPaw may use Live Activities to display travel-day information on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island when enabled.

TravelPaw Plus and Live Flight Refresh

TravelPaw Plus is planned for live flight refresh and airport detail updates. When live refresh is used, flight identifiers and trip timing needed to request flight status may be sent to flight data providers.

No Advertising or Tracking

TravelPaw does not use your trip data for advertising. TravelPaw does not sell your personal information.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes data stored locally on your device. If you created iCloud Drive backup files, you can delete those separately from iCloud Drive.

Children's Privacy

TravelPaw does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
