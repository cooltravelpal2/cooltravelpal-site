CardPecker Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

CardPecker is developed by TravelPal LLC. This policy describes how CardPecker 1.4 and later handles your data.

Information You Store in CardPecker

CardPecker stores the information you enter in its local app database. This may include card names and reward rules, spending categories, benefits and usage, receipts and attached images, rewards balances, and card-linked offers. CardPecker does not ask for or store full card numbers, bank passwords, or login credentials.

TravelPal Does Not Collect Your App Data

CardPecker has no TravelPal account or external CardPecker data server. TravelPal does not receive or have access to your cards, receipts, balances, offers, location, or backup files. The app does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking technology, and TravelPal does not sell or share your app data.

Receipt Scanning and Photos

If you choose to scan or attach a receipt, CardPecker requests access to the camera or the photos you select. Text recognition runs on your device using Apple frameworks. Receipt images you keep are stored with the app's local files and may be included in a backup.

Nearby Offers and Location

If you open Nearby Offers and grant location permission, CardPecker uses your current location to search for nearby merchant locations with Apple Maps services. CardPecker requests location only while you use the app, does not store your coordinates, and does not send them to TravelPal. Apple may process the search request under Apple's privacy policy. You can deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings.

Safari Sync

CardPecker Sync is an optional Safari extension. When enabled for a supported bank or loyalty website, it can read the rewards balance and offer text displayed on a page where you have already signed in. That extracted information is passed to CardPecker through an on-device App Group. The extension does not read or store passwords, cookies, or authentication tokens, and it does not send the extracted information to TravelPal. You control the extension and its website permissions in iOS Settings.

Backups and iCloud Drive

CardPecker can create backup files containing your app data, including attached receipt images. Manual backups are saved to the Files location you choose. Automatic backups are saved to your own iCloud Drive through Apple's iCloud service; the default frequency is weekly, and you can change it to Off, Daily, Weekly, or Monthly under Settings → Backup & Restore. TravelPal cannot access these files. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

No Tracking

CardPecker does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising identifiers, or any form of tracking.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete individual records or uninstall CardPecker. Uninstalling removes the app's local database and files. Backup files saved in Files or iCloud Drive are separate and remain until you delete them from those locations. You can disable automatic backup at any time and manage Safari Sync and location permissions in iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

CardPecker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email support@cooltravelpal.com or visit our support page.
