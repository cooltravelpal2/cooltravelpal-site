CardPecker Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

CardPecker is developed by TravelPal. This policy describes how CardPecker handles your data.

Data Collection

CardPecker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter — including credit cards, categories, and credit usage — is stored locally on your device only.

No Internet Connection

CardPecker does not connect to the internet. No data is sent to any server, third party, or analytics service.

No Tracking

CardPecker does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising identifiers, or any form of tracking.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling CardPecker permanently deletes all data stored by the app.

Children's Privacy

CardPecker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
