Privacy, plainly

  • What's stored: the cards, reward categories, and credit usage you enter — locally on your device only.
  • How data gets in: you enter it manually. Adding a card takes a couple of minutes, once.
  • Bank credentials: never requested, never stored. CardPecker doesn't connect to your accounts — it doesn't even need your card numbers.

Frequently asked questions

Does CardPecker connect to my bank?

No. CardPecker never asks for bank credentials and never connects to your accounts. You add your cards and their reward categories manually — no card numbers needed.

How does it know which card earns the most?

You record each card's earning rates by category. When you tap a category, CardPecker compares the effective return across your cards and shows the winner — including subcategory rules like specific airlines or hotel brands.

How do credits and offers work?

The Coupon Book tracks statement credits and recurring benefits and auto-refreshes them each billing period, so expiring credits surface before the clock runs out. Pair it with our benefit-tracking guide.

Does anything sync or leave my device?

CardPecker is built local-first: your data is stored on your device, with no account and no tracking. See the privacy policy for the details.

Is CardPecker financial advice?

No. CardPecker is an organizational tool — it compares the reward rules you enter. It doesn't recommend cards, and nothing on this page is financial, legal, or tax advice.
